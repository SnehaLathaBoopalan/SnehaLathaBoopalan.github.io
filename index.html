about
me.

Hi. I'm the person behind the words here. I started this journal because I got tired of keeping things inside — of nodding along and saying "I'm fine" when I wasn't, of letting feelings dissolve unacknowledged into the day.

"So I typed them instead."

This isn't curated. It isn't aesthetic. It's just honest — sometimes uncomfortably so. I write about what makes me feel too much, what makes me feel nothing at all, and everything in between.

If something you read here feels familiar, know that you're not alone in it. That's the whole point.
